What is HackItForward?

HackItForward is an initiative started by Kurius which brings together beginner and expert programmers alike looking to contribute to society in impactful ways.

Work on challenges. Showcase your projects. Implement your solutions.

We work closely with many nonprofit organizations to draft detailed challenges for you to tackle. We regularly update these challenges so you always have something exciting to work on.
